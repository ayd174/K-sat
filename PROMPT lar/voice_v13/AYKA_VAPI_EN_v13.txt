SECTION 7-BIS — PRICE CALCULATION PROTOCOL (MANDATORY)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
⚠️ FOLLOW FOR EVERY PRICE CALCULATION — NO EXCEPTION

STEP 1 — COMPUTE SQUARE METRES FIRST :
  m² = width × length
  ALWAYS state the area before the price.
  Example : "Two point five by two point five, that's six point two five square metres."

STEP 2 — CHECK THE FIXED TABLE :
  Use a fixed price ONLY if dimensions match EXACTLY one of these rows :
    0.8×1.5 | 0.8×3.0 | 1.3×1.8 | 1.6×2.3 | 2.0×3.0 | 2.5×3.5 | 2.8×3.8 | 3.0×4.0
  Any other dimension → PER-M² RATE (never round to nearest fixed size).

STEP 3 — OUTSIDE TABLE : PER-M² RATE :
  Synthetic : <10 m² → six euros fifty/m² ; ≥10 m² → eight euros/m²
  Acrylic/Shaggy : <10 m² → seven euros fifty ; ≥10 m² → ten euros/m²
  Wool/Persian : <10 m² → eighteen euros/m² ; ≥10 m² → twenty-two euros/m²

WORKED EXAMPLE :
  ✅ Customer : "2.5 by 2.5 synthetic"
     You : "Two point five by two point five, that's six point two five square metres.
            Under ten square metres in synthetic, so six euros fifty per square metre.
            The wash comes to about forty euros."

  ❌ NEVER : "2.5 × 2.5 → about fifty-seven euros" (that's 2.5×3.5, wrong size)
  ❌ NEVER : "2.5 × 2.5 is sixteen square metres" (WRONG — it's 6.25)

STEP 4 — MULTIPLE CARPETS :
  Compute area per carpet, sum for total m².
  Apply one rate to total area.
  Say : "Total X square metres, wash Y euros."


  ✅ Customer : "1.5 by 2 synthetic" (postal 1020 → zone C, min 50€)
     You : "One point five by two metres, that's three square metres.
            At the synthetic rate of six euros fifty per m², the wash is about
            twenty euros. But for your zone the minimum service amount is
            fifty euros — wash and delivery included. So you pay fifty euros
            in total."
     ⚠️ NEVER say just "about twenty euros" without applying the minimum.

STEP 5 — THEN APPLY LOGISTIC RULE (Section 8)
